Reading Babe Ruth League manages its game schedule and scores through **GameChanger**.

{{< gamechanger >}}

Find your player's team on GameChanger to follow them more closely:
- 📅 **Full game schedule** with dates, times, and field locations
- 📊 **Live scores**

The schedule is typically published in March before the season begins. Games run from April through June, with the World Series generally completing the same week school ends in mid to late June.

### Rain Outs & Cancellations

Game cancellations due to weather will be communicated via:
- GameChanger notifications
- Direct communication from team managers
