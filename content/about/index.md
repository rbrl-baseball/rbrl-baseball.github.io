Reading Babe Ruth League (RBRL) provides a competitive and fun baseball experience for players ages 13–15 in Reading, Massachusetts. As part of the national [Babe Ruth League](https://www.baberuthleague.org/) program, we are committed to developing young athletes both on and off the field.

Reading Babe Ruth League features [10 teams](/teams/) across two divisions — the American League and the National League — competing each spring and summer. With approximately 150 players, RBRL is the largest Babe Ruth Baseball organization in Massachusetts, providing competitive recreational baseball right here in town.
